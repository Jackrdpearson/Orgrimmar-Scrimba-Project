Orgrimmar

Visit Orgrimmar today, enjoy a vibrant, multi-cultural city on the east coast of Kalimdor filled with never ending excitement and things to keep yourself occupied with.

Top three reasons to visit Orgrimmar

Great travel links!

Orgrimmar has incredible links to many other places in Kalimdor and beyond, with a bat rider master who can take you anywhere on the continent or the complimentary and luxurious zeppelin located just outside the city to take you beyond!

Unbelievable shopping district!

Orgrimmar has a fantastic district dedicated to only the finest goods, from artisan axes through to potent potions, you will find anything you could possibly need and even pick up some new skills along the way.

Beautiful historic architecture!

Orgrimmar has been a landmark in Kalimdor for many centuries and is home to the native populations, the orcs and trolls. They have developed unique traditions and this shows in the architecture, from the casle walls through to even the smallest shacks.
